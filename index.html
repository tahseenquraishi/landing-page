FAQs

What is Refyne?

Based in Bengaluru, Refyne is an innovative fintech company which provides employees of partnered companies the access to On-Demand Salary based on their attendance. The employees can withdraw their earned salary any time during the month.

What is earned salary?

When a person works for a company every day, their salary is accumulated over days, eventually getting paid on the salary day (typically end of the month). The salary amount proportional to the days worked is called earned salary.

What is the withdrawal limit?

Based on your attendance and monthly salary, we calculate your earned salary daily, which you can also see on Refyne app. We set a percentage limit on this earned salary as your withdrawal limit. Many employers start with 30% as the limit and increase that to their special and tenured employees. You can check this limit on the Refyne app Real-time.

What are the charges?

Unlike other financial products, we do not charge any interest, convenience or documentation charges. Refyne charges a simple, one-time processing fee that starts from Rs. 29 (GST included). You can see the processing fee and the entire breakup on the app at the time of withdrawal.

Why should I be charged?

Getting your own money, your earned salary before the salary date requires various partners like banking, technology, payroll to incur costs to make the facility available to all employees. The processing fee enables us to provide the service of On-Demand pay in your need of the hour and seamlessly.

What is the interest I need to pay?

Refyne does not charge any interest. You need to pay only a fixed, one-time processing fee that starts from Rs. 29 (GST included). You can see the processing fee and the entire breakup on the app at the time of withdrawal.

What is the process of joining?

Refyne ties up with companies that have agreed to extend On-Demand Salary to their employees. The company’s HR uploads the list of employees who need to be given this benefit. The employee can download Refyne app and sign up using the email ID provided by the company HR.

How much can I withdraw?

The amount that you can withdraw depends on salary earned by you. This depends on the number of days attended by you and the company’s withdrawal % limit. Typically companies set a withdrawal limit of 30% to 70% of the total earned salary.
